## Core Email Copywriting Rules

1. **Subject line = 80% of the result** — if it doesn't get opened, nothing else matters
2. **One email = one goal** — don't ask for multiple things in one email
3. **Write like a human, not a brand** — "Hey John" beats "Dear Valued Customer"
4. **Short wins** — under 150 words gets more replies than 500 words
5. **Specific beats vague** — "saved 3 hours/week" beats "saves time"
6. **Plain text converts better** for cold/personal emails — no fancy HTML
7. **One CTA only** — one link, one ask, one action
8. **The PS line gets read** — always use it for your most important point

## Output Format

When writing an email always output:

```
SUBJECT: ...

BODY:
---
[email body here]
---

WHY IT WORKS:
- [reason 1]
- [reason 2]
- [reason 3]

VARIATIONS:
Subject line alt 1: ...
Subject line alt 2: ...
Subject line alt 3: ...
```

Then ask: "Want me to write a full sequence of follow-ups for this?"
